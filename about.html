Our Backstory

Over an evening meal at an Upcountry Outing venue in October 2019, a group of five couples who all belonged to Watoto Church and were part of a Marrieds’ small group discussed ways in which they could collectively do a commercial activity together. This was to empower them financially in addition to keeping them together as they did life together.

Upon agreement in that direction, the decision now was on the choice of the venture or business that they could undertake. Among the couples, there was one couple – the Kyewas, who were in the Gardening and Landscaping business and would regularly consume pavers and slabs as part of their work. They desired to be able to get a quality and consistent supply of such products as a great addition to their landscaping business. This pointed to the rest of the couples that there was an opportunity in the concrete products business, starting with pavers, but also growing into others such as solid and hollow blocks, Kerb stones of different sizes.

The next step was the formalization of the idea through company registration formalities and active seeking of the infrastructure (machines, space, start-up capital, human resources) that were necessary for the kickoff of the business venture. In August 2021, most of the prerequisites for the establishment of the machine/factory were met, and Infinite Concrete Investments Ltd started production at the Matugga site.

As of today, the company has moved from the Matugga site to Kiti along the Matugga-Kiti road and further expanded to Mbarara, where they operate a similar business. The current product range comprises 6-inch hollow and solid blocks, 8-inch hollow and solid blocks, 60mm domestic and 80mm commercial pavers of different patterns and kerbstones.
